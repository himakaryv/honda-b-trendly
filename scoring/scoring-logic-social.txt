Scoring Framework for News, Google Trends, and Social Media Listening


Credibility Scoring (Weight: 25%) X Frequency Scoring (Weight: 30%) X  Recency Scoring (Weight: 25%) x Relevance Scoring (Weight: 20%)


Credibility = (News Authority × 0.3) + (Social Authority × 0.3) + (Search Quality × 0.25) + (Verification × 0.15)
Frequency = (News × 0.25) + (LinkedIn × 0.2) + (Twitter × 0.2) + (Reddit × 0.15) + (Search × 0.2)
Recency = (News × 0.25) + (Social Momentum × 0.35) + (Search × 0.25) + (Platform Convergence × 0.15)
Relevance = (Industry Context × 0.3) + (Social Sentiment × 0.35) + (Commercial Intent × 0.2) + (Actionability × 0.15)




1. Credibility Scoring (Weight: 25%)
News Source Authority Assessment (0-10 scale):
* Tier 1 (9-10 points): Global business media (WSJ, FT, Bloomberg, Reuters, The Economist)
* Tier 2 (7-8 points): National majors (NYT, WaPo), specialized trade media (TechCrunch, Industry Week)
* Tier 3 (5-6 points): Regional business press, industry newsletters, credible digital natives
* Tier 4 (3-4 points): Aggregators, corporate newsrooms, emerging media outlets
* Tier 5 (1-2 points): Unverified blogs, social media posts, content farms
Social Media Authority Signals:
* LinkedIn (Professional weight: 9-10 points):
   * C-suite executives discussing trend: +3 bonus
   * Industry thought leaders (>50K followers) posting: +2 bonus
   * Company pages announcing initiatives: +2 bonus
   * Professional groups with >10K members discussing: +1 bonus
* X/Twitter (Influencer weight: 7-8 points):
   * Verified industry accounts discussing: +2 bonus
   * Threading with data/analysis (not just opinions): +2 bonus
   * Tech/Business journalists amplifying: +1 bonus
   * Viral professional threads (>1K retweets): +1 bonus
* Reddit (Community weight: 6-7 points):
   * Multiple subreddit discussions (r/technology, r/business, etc.): +2 bonus
   * High-quality posts with sources/data: +2 bonus
   * Industry-specific subreddit validation: +1 bonus
   * AMA with industry experts: +1 bonus
Google Trends Signal Quality:
* High confidence (9-10 points): Clear sustained pattern over 12+ months
* Strong signal (7-8 points): Consistent growth over 6-12 months
* Moderate signal (5-6 points): Emerging pattern over 3-6 months
* Weak signal (3-4 points): Volatile but upward trend
* Noise (1-2 points): Sporadic spikes without pattern
Cross-Platform Verification:
* Consensus across news + search + social: +3 bonus
* Professional (LinkedIn) validates consumer (Reddit) interest: +2 bonus
* Geographic consistency across platforms: +1 bonus
* Influencer and grassroots alignment: +1 bonus


2. Frequency Scoring (Weight: 30%)
News Volume Metrics:
* Saturation coverage (10 points): >100 articles/week across major outlets
* Heavy coverage (8 points): 50-100 articles/week
* Moderate coverage (6 points): 20-50 articles/week
* Emerging coverage (4 points): 5-20 articles/week
* Sporadic mentions (2 points): <5 articles/week
Social Media Volume Metrics:
LinkedIn Engagement:
* Viral professional (10 points): >500 posts/week, >100K total engagement
* High buzz (8 points): 200-500 posts/week from professionals
* Growing discussion (6 points): 50-200 posts/week
* Niche conversation (4 points): 10-50 posts/week
* Minimal presence (2 points): <10 posts/week
X/Twitter Velocity:
* Trending topic (10 points): Multiple hashtags trending, >10K tweets/day
* High velocity (8 points): 5-10K tweets/day
* Active discussion (6 points): 1-5K tweets/day
* Moderate chatter (4 points): 100-1K tweets/day
* Low buzz (2 points): <100 tweets/day
Reddit Engagement:
* Front page material (10 points): Multiple posts with >1K upvotes
* Subreddit hot topic (8 points): Top posts in relevant subreddits
* Active threads (6 points): 10+ active discussions
* Emerging topic (4 points): 3-10 threads with engagement
* Sporadic mentions (2 points): Occasional comments only
Google Trends Search Intensity:
* Breakout term (10 points): >5000% increase, labeled "Breakout"
* Rapid growth (8 points): 500-5000% increase
* Strong growth (6 points): 100-500% increase
* Steady growth (4 points): 25-100% increase
* Slight increase (2 points): <25% increase


3. Recency Scoring (Weight: 25%)
News Temporal Dynamics:
* Breaking/Accelerating (10 points): Hourly updates, breaking news alerts
* Hot topic (8 points): Daily coverage with new developments
* Active story (6 points): Weekly updates and analysis pieces
* Periodic coverage (4 points): Monthly feature articles
* Historical reference (2 points): Quarterly or less frequent
Social Media Momentum Indicators:
LinkedIn Signals:
* Weekly "pulse" articles on topic: +2 bonus
* LinkedIn News featuring trend: +2 bonus
* Increasing executive commentary: +1 bonus
* LinkedIn Learning courses launched: +1 bonus
X/Twitter Momentum:
* Spaces/audio discussions scheduled: +2 bonus
* Influential accounts just joining conversation: +2 bonus
* Hashtag velocity increasing >50% week-over-week: +1 bonus
* Cross-industry discussion emerging: +1 bonus
Reddit Evolution:
* New subreddit created for topic: +2 bonus
* Pinned threads/megathreads created: +2 bonus
* Wiki/resource pages being built: +1 bonus
* Cross-posting across multiple subreddits: +1 bonus
Search Trend Momentum:
* Vertical spike (10 points): Current month is peak, sharp acceleration
* Strong momentum (8 points): 3-month moving average rising >50%
* Building momentum (6 points): 6-month trend positive
* Gradual rise (4 points): 12-month trend positive but slow
* Plateau/declining (2 points): Flat or negative recent movement
Recency Score Formula:
Recency = (News × 0.25) + (Social Momentum × 0.35) + (Search × 0.25) + (Platform Convergence × 0.15)
4. Relevance Scoring (Weight: 20%)
Industry Context Alignment:
* Direct industry focus (10 points): Specifically about your sector
* Strong relevance (8 points): Clear implications discussed
* Applicable insights (6 points): Transferable to your context
* Indirect relevance (4 points): Requires interpretation
* Peripheral (2 points): Minimal connection
Social Sentiment Quality:
LinkedIn Professional Relevance:
* Industry leaders implementing: +3 bonus
* Case studies shared: +2 bonus
* Job postings mentioning trend: +2 bonus
* Skills courses trending: +1 bonus
X/Twitter Commercial Signals:
* Companies announcing adoption: +2 bonus
* Customer success stories: +2 bonus
* Competitor mentions increasing: +1 bonus
* Industry analysts covering: +1 bonus
Reddit Practical Discussion:
* r/[YourIndustry] actively discussing: +2 bonus
* Implementation guides shared: +2 bonus
* Problem-solving threads: +1 bonus
* Tool recommendations: +1 bonus
Search Query Commercial Intent:
* Transactional queries (10 points): "buy," "price," "cost" + term
* Commercial investigation (8 points): "best," "review," "comparison"
* Informational with intent (6 points): "how to," "guide," "tutorial"
* General informational (4 points): "what is," "definition"
* Awareness only (2 points): Brand or concept only
Relevance Score Formula:
Relevance = (Industry Context × 0.3) + (Social Sentiment × 0.35) + (Commercial Intent × 0.2) + (Actionability × 0.15)
